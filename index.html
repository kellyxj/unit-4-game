Crystal Collector

You will be given a goal number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. Each crystal will increase your score by a fixed amount when clicked.
You win the game by matching your score to the goal number. You lose if you go over the goal number.
The value of each crystal will be hidden until you click on it.
Whenever the game starts, the values of the crystals will be randomized.
